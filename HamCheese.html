HAM & CHEESE

    Get a Toast Take one, two or more slices of cheese and put on the toast Take one, two or more slices of ham and put on the toast If you use more than one slice of each rotate the cheese and ham Turn on a pan middle heat Put butter on the outside of the toast, on both toast slices Put the toast into the pan and cover with a lit Asap the butter on top is melted check if you can turn the toast, it should be nice brown on the bottom and inside the cheese is melted When the toast is turned don't cover the pan anymore so the brown layer keeps crusty After few minutes check if the toast ist brown on the bottom If he is brown take it out and enjoy
